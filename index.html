Halloween Graveyard Cake:

Ingredients:

  • Chocolate cake (baked and cooled)
  • Chocolate frosting
  • Crushed chocolate cookies (for dirt)
  • Assorted Halloween-themed candies and gummy worms
  • Edible tombstone decorations (made from chocolate or fondant)

Instructions:

1

✻

Frost the chocolate cake with chocolate frosting.

2

✻

Sprinkle the crushed chocolate cookies over the frosting to create a "dirt" layer.

3

✻

Decorate the cake with Halloween candies, creating a spooky graveyard scene.

4

✻

Place the edible tombstone decorations in the cake, sticking them into the frosting.


5

✻

Add gummy worms crawling out of the "graves" for an extra creepy touch.




Delicious Pies:

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups peeled, cored, and sliced apples (such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 1 package refrigerated pie crusts or homemade pie crust (for top and bottom crust)

Instructions:

1

✻

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).

2

✻

In a large mixing bowl, combine sliced apples, sugar, lemon juice, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Toss until the apples are evenly coated.

3

✻

Line a 9-inch pie pan with one one of the pie crusts. Pour the apple mixture into the crust.

4

✻

Dot the apples with butter.

5

✻

Cover the apples with the second pie crust. You can create a lattice crust or a full crust with slits for ventilation. Seal and crimp the edges.

6

✻

Optional: Brush the top crust with milk or beaten egg and sprinkle with a little sugar for a golden, crispy finish.

7

✻

Place the pie on a baking sheet to catch any drips and bake for 45-55 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

8

✻

Allow the pie to cool before serving.

No-Bake Chocolate Slice:

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups crushed biscuits (e.g., cookies, digestive biscuits)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (such as walnuts or almonds)
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

1

✻

In a medium bowl, mix crushed biscuits, powdered sugar, chopped nuts, and cocoa powder.

2

✻

Add melted butter and vanilla extract to the mixture, and combine well.

3

✻

Press the mixture into a lined tray or dish and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until set.

4

✻

Once set, slice and serve.
